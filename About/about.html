About Gymnasium

The Gymnasium at Central Institute of Technology, Kokrajhar is a premier fitness hub, providing world-class facilities to students, professors, and fitness enthusiasts. Designed to cater to all fitness levels, our gym fosters a culture of health, discipline, and strength.

Our Mission

We aim to empower individuals by promoting a fitness-oriented lifestyle through guided workouts, endurance training, strength-building, and wellness programs.

Facilities & Programs

  • State-of-the-Art Equipment
  • Professional Trainers
  • Group Fitness Sessions
  • Personalized Training Programs
  • Yoga & Wellness Sessions

Why Choose Us?

Our fitness community is inclusive and welcoming to all. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced athlete, we provide customized training programs to help you reach your fitness goals effectively.

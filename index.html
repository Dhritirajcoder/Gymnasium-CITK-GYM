Our Legacy

The gymnasium at Central Institute of Technology, Kokrajhar stands as a premier fitness hub, offering world-class facilities to students, professors, and fitness enthusiasts. Designed to cater to individuals of all fitness levels, our gym fosters a culture of health, discipline, and strength.Beyond just workouts, our fitness programs focus on overall wellness, including endurance training, strength-building, and guided sessions. Our inclusive environment ensures that everyone—from beginners to professionals—can achieve their fitness goals effectively.
